LEARN TO SURF WITH US

We are most of all a surf school, but we also provide lots of other activities in the local area. We
are so fortunate to live in a place with so many water adventure activities available, so we want
you to have the option to experience them by our side. Marco is a waterman and adventure
specialist with years of experience in the area; he is ready to help you!

Friendly, qualified, multilingual instuctors

We have a fantastic team of instructors, many of whom have been with the Aloha Surf School
family for years! Our instructors are from all over the world and speak various languages. We
focus especially on communicating with our students: young, old, small groups and large groups.
We want you to feel like part of the family. And, apart from learning to surf, we want you to be
safe, smile, laugh and have 100% fun.
